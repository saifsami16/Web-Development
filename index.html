Tesla Roadster

The quickest car in the world, with the fastest acceleration, speed and performance. The Tesla Roadster is a battery electric vehicle sports car that is it runs completely on its electrical charge without producing any combustion of fuel, hence is highly environment friendly. The Tesla Roadster can travel 620 miles on a single charge and can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in 1.9 seconds.

Rolls Royce Phantom VIII

The Rolls-Royce Phantom VIII is a luxurious car designed and manufactured by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. It is said to be the most luxurious car present on the surface of the earth. The interior is fully bespoke and contains customized art gallery like painting through which you can set the color or design of almost half of your dashboard of your own choosing and can change it whenever you want. Furthermore, it has a complete LED filled interior roof. You can set the brightness of these LED’s yourself which will give you a look alike of pretty beautiful starry night sky. The car seats and the steering part are completely covered in leather. There are panels at the rear of the front seat which contains Screens in them. There is also a hand-rest in the middle of the rear seats. The trunk has been made very spacey and can contain a lot of luggage to move around.

Mileage (upto) 9.8 kmpl
Engine (upto) 6749 cc
BHP 453.0

Lamborghini Veneno

The Lamborghini Veneno is a limited production high performance sports car manufactured by Lamborghini. The Veneno was designed and manufactured to celebrate Lamborghini’s 50th anniversary. When introduced in 2013, it had a price of US$4,500,000 and this makes it the one of the most expensive cars ever manufactured till date in the world. The Lamborghini Veneno has a top limit and highest speed of 356 km/h and it also has a 0–97 km/h acceleration time within a short time of 2.8 seconds. The car has a braking distance of unbelievable few 30 m from 97–0 km/h which is truly amazing. The roadster went on sale for €3,300,000 without tax. Only nine cars of the roadster were ever produced and all of them were already sold before there manufacturing as soon as they were announced
